# Season Clock Card

![Season Clock Card preview](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/axelfair/seasonclock/main/assets/preview.png)

Season Clock Card is a polished Home Assistant Lovelace card that shows a premium, location-aware seasonal year clock for your dashboard.

It includes compact solstice/equinox markers, daily and monthly tick marks, a moon phase hand badge, weather icon and temperature complication, configurable clock-face elements, and Northern/Southern Hemisphere support based on Home Assistant home location, a selected entity, manual coordinates, or explicit hemisphere selection.
